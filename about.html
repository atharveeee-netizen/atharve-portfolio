You look.
And look again.

Lying on the floor, probe in hand, board upside down. Atharve looks for more than what the schematic shows. Because every detail counts. Every trace carries a decision with it, and every corner hides something worth understanding.

Each build carries memories: simply lovely.

"I am not, yet you are." A line I keep close. Electronics engineer, VLSI student, builder of things that fly and things that think.

I wanted only to live in accord with the promptings of my true self.Hesse, Demian

Trust as a starting point

I don't chase a grand plan, Camus would say there isn't one. I follow what's interesting until it works, and lately that's the coldest, quietest hardware there is: cryogenics, microwave lines, the strange silicon quantum machines run on. Why this, why now? Blame the sun.

Man is the only creature that refuses to be what he is.Camus, The Rebel

We listen.
Really.

To the problem before the build. A drone is easy. A drone that finds a person under rubble is the only version worth making.
