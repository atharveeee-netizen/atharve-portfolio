## Part 2 — Voice Bible v1

**Three adjectives:** measured · surreal-at-the-edges · close-to-the-metal.

**Always:** (1) end the line on the power word; (2) one concrete noun or real number per claim; (3) active voice — the builder acts, the machine flies; (4) stay inside two semantic fields: flight/nature, engineering/precision; (5) remove a word; if nothing breaks, remove another.

**Never:** (1) exclamation marks or rhyme; (2) "Meet X", "behind the scenes", "best experience", "ideas into reality" or anything from the banned list; (3) invented facts, metrics, or quotes; (4) louder than the two locked quotes; (5) explain a joke or a silence.

**Three example lines:** "Cold kills noise. That's where I work." · "The bench, as found." · "Coming soon — props balanced, the story still drying."
